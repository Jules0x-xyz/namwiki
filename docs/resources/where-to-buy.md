# Where to Buy NAM

A comprehensive list of places where you can purchase NAM tokens.

:::warning Disclaimer
This information is for educational purposes only. Always do your own research before using any exchange. Exchange availability varies by region.
:::

## Centralized Exchanges (CEX)

Centralized exchanges offer easy fiat on-ramps and liquidity.

### Major Exchanges

| Exchange | Pairs | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| *Coming Soon* | - | Check official announcements |

*Exchange listings are updated as they become available.*

### How to Buy on CEX

1. Create and verify account
2. Deposit funds (fiat or crypto)
3. Navigate to NAM trading pair
4. Place buy order
5. Withdraw to your wallet

:::tip Security
- Always enable 2FA
- Withdraw to personal wallet
- Don't store long-term on exchanges
:::

## Decentralized Exchanges (DEX)

Trade without KYC or custody risk.

### Available DEXs

| DEX | Chain | Features |
|-----|-------|----------|
| Osmosis | Cosmos | IBC, swaps |

### How to Buy on DEX

1. Connect wallet (Keplr/Namada Extension)
2. Have source tokens (ATOM, OSMO, USDC)
3. Navigate to NAM pair
4. Set swap parameters
5. Confirm transaction

### DEX Advantages

- ✅ No KYC required
- ✅ Non-custodial
- ✅ Often lower fees
- ✅ Immediate access

### DEX Considerations

- ⚠️ Requires existing crypto
- ⚠️ Slippage on large orders
- ⚠️ No fiat on-ramp

## IBC Swaps

If you have tokens on other Cosmos chains:

### Process

```
1. Have ATOM/OSMO/etc. on source chain
2. IBC to DEX (e.g., Osmosis)
3. Swap to NAM
4. IBC to Namada
```

### Supported Source Chains

| Chain | Token | Path |
|-------|-------|------|
| Cosmos Hub | ATOM | ATOM → Osmosis → NAM → Namada |
| Osmosis | OSMO | OSMO → NAM → Namada |
| Other IBC chains | Various | Chain → Osmosis → NAM → Namada |

## Buying Tips

### Do

- ✅ Compare fees across platforms
- ✅ Check withdrawal limits
- ✅ Verify withdrawal network (Namada!)
- ✅ Start with small test transactions
- ✅ Use 2FA everywhere

### Don't

- ❌ Share your credentials
- ❌ Keep large amounts on exchanges
- ❌ Send to wrong network
- ❌ Skip verification steps
