# About NamWiki

NamWiki is a community-driven educational resource for the Namada blockchain ecosystem.

## Our Mission

**Make Namada education accessible to everyone.**

We believe that understanding privacy technology shouldn't require a PhD in cryptography. NamWiki breaks down complex concepts into understandable guides, tutorials, and explanations.
