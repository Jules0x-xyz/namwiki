Buy NAM

This guide covers all the ways to acquire NAM tokens, from centralized exchanges to decentralized options.

Quick Options​

MethodBest ForSpeedFees
CEXLarge amountsFastLow-Medium
DEXPrivacy-consciousMediumLow
IBC SwapCosmos usersFastLow

Centralized Exchanges (CEX)​

Tier 1 Exchanges​

Major exchanges that list NAM:

Exchange Listings

Exchange availability varies by region. Check each exchange for your country's availability.

ExchangePairsFeatures
Exchange ANAM/USDT, NAM/BTCLowest fees
Exchange BNAM/USDTInstant deposits
Exchange CNAM/USDFiat on-ramp

How to Buy on a CEX​

  1. Create an account on a supported exchange
  2. Complete KYC (identity verification)
  3. Deposit funds (fiat or crypto)
  4. Buy NAM using market or limit orders
  5. Withdraw to your wallet (see next section)

Withdrawing from Exchange​

After purchasing:

  1. Go to Withdraw section
  2. Select NAM as the asset
  3. Enter your tnam address
  4. Choose Namada network (not ERC-20!)
  5. Confirm withdrawal
  6. Wait for processing (varies by exchange)
Network Warning

Always select the Namada network when withdrawing. Sending to wrong network = lost funds.

Decentralized Exchanges (DEX)​

Benefits of DEX​

  • ✅ No KYC required
  • ✅ Non-custodial
  • ✅ More privacy
  • ✅ Direct to wallet

Available DEXs​

DEXTypeAccess
OsmosisIBCosmosis.zone
AstroportIBCastroport.fi

How to Buy on DEX​

  1. Connect your wallet (Keplr or Namada Extension)
  2. Have source tokens ready (ATOM, OSMO, USDC)
  3. Select swap: SOURCE → NAM
  4. Review rate and slippage
  5. Confirm swap
  6. Receive NAM in your wallet

IBC Transfers​

If you already have tokens on other Cosmos chains:

From Osmosis​

1. Have OSMO or other tokens on Osmosis
2. Swap to NAM on Osmosis DEX
3. IBC transfer to Namada

From Cosmos Hub​

1. Have ATOM on Cosmos Hub
2. IBC to Osmosis
3. Swap ATOM → NAM
4. IBC NAM to Namada

See IBC Transfers Guide for detailed steps.

Price Tracking​

Track NAM price on:

  • CoinGecko: coingecko.com/namada
  • CoinMarketCap: coinmarketcap.com
  • DeFiLlama: defillama.com

Tax Considerations​

Remember that buying crypto may have tax implications:

  • Keep records of purchases
  • Note the date and price
  • Consult a tax professional
Not Financial Advice

This guide is educational only. Always do your own research before purchasing any cryptocurrency.

Security Tips​

Before Buying​

  • Research the exchange/DEX
  • Enable 2FA on exchange accounts
  • Have your wallet ready

During Purchase​

  • Double-check amounts
  • Review fees
  • Start with a small test amount

After Buying​

  • Withdraw to your personal wallet
  • Verify receipt
  • Consider shielding for privacy

Common Issues​

"NAM not showing after withdrawal"​

  • Check transaction on block explorer
  • Wait for network confirmations
  • Verify you used correct network

"High withdrawal fees"​

  • Compare exchange fee schedules
  • Consider larger, less frequent withdrawals
  • DEX options often have lower fees

"Exchange not available in my country"​

  • Try VPN (check exchange ToS)
  • Use a DEX instead
  • IBC from another Cosmos chain

After Buying NAM​

Once you have NAM in your wallet:

  1. Send to Your Wallet - Secure your tokens
  2. Shield Your Assets - Enable privacy
  3. Start Staking - Earn rewards
