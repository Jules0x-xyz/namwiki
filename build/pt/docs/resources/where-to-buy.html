Where to Buy NAM

A comprehensive list of places where you can purchase NAM tokens.

Disclaimer

This information is for educational purposes only. Always do your own research before using any exchange. Exchange availability varies by region.

Centralized Exchanges (CEX)​

Centralized exchanges offer easy fiat on-ramps and liquidity.

Major Exchanges​

ExchangePairsNotes
Coming Soon-Check official announcements

Exchange listings are updated as they become available.

How to Buy on CEX​

  1. Create and verify account
  2. Deposit funds (fiat or crypto)
  3. Navigate to NAM trading pair
  4. Place buy order
  5. Withdraw to your wallet
Security
  • Always enable 2FA
  • Withdraw to personal wallet
  • Don't store long-term on exchanges

Decentralized Exchanges (DEX)​

Trade without KYC or custody risk.

Available DEXs​

DEXChainFeatures
OsmosisCosmosIBC, swaps

How to Buy on DEX​

  1. Connect wallet (Keplr/Namada Extension)
  2. Have source tokens (ATOM, OSMO, USDC)
  3. Navigate to NAM pair
  4. Set swap parameters
  5. Confirm transaction

DEX Advantages​

  • ✅ No KYC required
  • ✅ Non-custodial
  • ✅ Often lower fees
  • ✅ Immediate access

DEX Considerations​

  • ⚠️ Requires existing crypto
  • ⚠️ Slippage on large orders
  • ⚠️ No fiat on-ramp

IBC Swaps​

If you have tokens on other Cosmos chains:

Process​

1. Have ATOM/OSMO/etc. on source chain
2. IBC to DEX (e.g., Osmosis)
3. Swap to NAM
4. IBC to Namada

Supported Source Chains​

ChainTokenPath
Cosmos HubATOMATOM → Osmosis → NAM → Namada
OsmosisOSMOOSMO → NAM → Namada
Other IBC chainsVariousChain → Osmosis → NAM → Namada

Peer-to-Peer (P2P)​

Buy directly from other users:

Platforms​

PlatformNotes
Local communitiesDiscord, Telegram
P2P platformsComing soon

P2P Safety​

  • ⚠️ Use escrow when possible
  • ⚠️ Verify transaction before releasing funds
  • ⚠️ Trade with reputable community members
  • ⚠️ Start with small amounts

OTC (Over-the-Counter)​

For large purchases:

Options​

  • Contact major market makers
  • Institutional desks
  • Community OTC channels

Benefits​

  • Better rates for large amounts
  • Less slippage
  • Personalized service

Fiat On-Ramps​

Buy crypto with fiat currency:

Options​

  1. Buy ATOM/USDC first: Many on-ramps support these
  2. Swap to NAM: Use DEX or CEX

Common Fiat Methods​

MethodSpeedFees
Bank transferSlow (1-5 days)Low
Credit cardFast (instant)High (3-5%)
Wire transferMedium (1-3 days)Medium

Regional Availability​

Exchange availability varies by location:

RegionCommon Options
North AmericaVaries

OtherDEX recommended

Check each exchange for your country's availability.

After Purchasing​

Once you have NAM:

  1. Withdraw to wallet - Don't leave on exchange
  2. Shield for privacy - Use Shield Assets
  3. Consider staking - How to Stake

Buying Tips​

Do​

  • ✅ Compare fees across platforms
  • ✅ Check withdrawal limits
  • ✅ Verify withdrawal network (Namada!)
  • ✅ Start with small test transactions
  • ✅ Use 2FA everywhere

Don't​

  • ❌ Share your credentials
  • ❌ Keep large amounts on exchanges
  • ❌ Send to wrong network
  • ❌ Skip verification steps

Not Financial Advice
